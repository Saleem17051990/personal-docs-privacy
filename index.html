Introduction

Personal Docs Manager ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Personal Docs Manager collects the following information:

  • Google Account Information: Your Google account email address and profile information (name, profile picture) for authentication purposes.
  • Google Drive Access: Access to documents stored in your Google Drive account. We only access files that you explicitly select or authorize through the app.
  • App Usage Data: Basic usage information to improve app functionality.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Authenticate your identity and provide secure access to the app
  • Access and manage your documents in Google Drive as requested by you
  • Provide document management features and functionality
  • Improve our app's performance and user experience

Data Storage

All your documents and files remain stored in your Google Drive account. We do not store, copy, or retain your documents on our servers. Your data stays in your Google Drive, and we only access it when you explicitly request it through the app.

Data Security

We use Google's secure OAuth 2.0 authentication system to protect your data. All API calls are encrypted using industry-standard security protocols. We do not store your Google account credentials.

Third-Party Services

Our app uses Google Drive API to access your documents. Your use of Google Drive is also subject to Google's Privacy Policy, which can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Revoke app access to your Google Drive at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Uninstall the app, which will remove all app-related permissions
